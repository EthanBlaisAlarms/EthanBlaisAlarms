EthanBlaisAlarms Software Support - Bans

This page will detail EBA Software bans.

What does a ban do?

When EthanBlaisAlarms Software bans a user, that user will not be allowed to access our services for the duration of the ban. If a specific device has been banned, switching users will not bypass the ban; the entire device is banned.

Why does EBA Software ban?

EthanBlaisAlarms Software will ban users that violate the EULA. EBA Software reserves the right to ban any user or device for any reason.

How long will bans last?

In general, bans will be 14 days for first-time offenders. Subsequent bans may last longer. Keep in mind, the ban length may depend on various other factors. Severe violations can result in a permanent ban.

Will bans affect my software data?

No. Your software data will remain intact for the duration of the ban.

Why was I banned?

The ban reason and expiration date will be displayed when attempting to load our software.

I was falsely banned!

If you believe your ban is false, you may contact EthanBlaisAlarms Software.
